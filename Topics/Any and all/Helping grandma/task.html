Helping grandma

Your grandma really likes gardening. Her plants and flowers are known in the whole neighborhood, and she is really proud of them. This month has been unusually chilly, and she needs to know when to take some of her pots inside. She's asked you to look at the weather
forecast for the next week and tell her if any of the nights the temperature will drop below 0Â°C to prepare for the relocation of the plants.

Now, of course, you don't need to write a program to look at the weekly weather forecast, but it is a good chance to practice any and all! Look at the lowest night temperatures for the following week:

lowest_temperatures = [+2, +4, -1, +0.5, -2, 0, -1]

Positive temperatures are given with the plus sign, but it's merely for convenience. Choose what any(lowest_temperatures) and all(lowest_temperatures) functions will return in this case.
